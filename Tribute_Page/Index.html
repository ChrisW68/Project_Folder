Albert Einstein

Life events of Einstein:

  • Born in 1879
  • 1884 - While sick at home, sees a compass for first time and made him curious about the forces.
  • 1894 - Albert's family moves to Milan, Italy, but Albert stayed to finish his schooling. Although at the end of term he quits school and reunites with family.
  • 1895 - Albert moves to Aarau, Switzerland to enter the Feceral Polythenical Institute in Zurich. He writes his first scientific essay, "On the Investigation of the State of Ether in a Magnetic Field."
  • 1903 - Marries Mileva.
  • 1904 - Albert's son,Hans Albert is born
  • 1905 - Publishes his work on Brownian motion, ideas about light, the Special Theory of Relativity. He receives his Ph.D. and creates the famous equation: E=mc2.
  • 1916 - Albert General Theory of Relativity is finally published.
  • 1919 - Albert marries Elsa.
  • 1923 - Albert receives Nobel Prize for Physics.
  • 1934 - Albert visits Franklin Roosevelt; his daughter Ilse dies.
  • 1936 - Elsa dies.
  • 1939 - Einstein writes to Roosevelt warning about the danger of the atom bomb being discovered by Hilter's Germany
  • 1940 - Manhattan Project starts in USA. Einstein becomes US citizen.
  • 1945 - U.S. drops two atom bombs inJapan and Ends the World War II.
  • 1955 - Albert dies with a half finished equation next to his bed.
